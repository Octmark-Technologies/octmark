## The challenge

The team was generating inbound leads at a healthy volume. The problem: nobody could tell which ones were worth chasing. Sales spent equal time on a ₹5L opportunity and a ₹20k inquiry. The CRM had been configured once and never revisited. No lead scoring. No qualification criteria. Revenue data lived in a spreadsheet.

The result was a pipeline number that looked good and meant nothing.

## What we built

We started by mapping every lead source against actual closed revenue for the previous 18 months. The pattern was immediate: two channels produced 82% of closed revenue but represented only 31% of leads. The team was spending 69% of their effort on sources that almost never converted.

We built a qualification model with three tiers, immediate follow-up, nurture sequence, and no-action, and trained the CRM to assign them automatically. AI screening was layered across the two high-performing channels to pre-qualify before the lead reached the team at all.

Revenue reporting was unified for the first time: a single dashboard connecting source to stage to close to lifetime value.

## The outcome

Within 90 days, qualified pipeline, leads the team was confident in, grew 2.8× while total lead volume was unchanged. Time to qualified status dropped 38% because the team wasn't spending time on impossible-to-convert leads. Revenue attribution meant the team could finally make investment decisions based on evidence.
